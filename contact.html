Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

What services do you offer?

I specialize in frontend web development, including responsive website design, React applications, landing pages, and e-commerce solutions. I also work with backend technologies like PHP and MySQL for full-stack projects.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and requirements. A simple landing page might take 1-2 weeks, while a complex web application could take 1-3 months. I always provide detailed timelines during project planning.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide. I'm comfortable with remote collaboration and have experience working across different time zones. Communication is primarily in English.

What is your development process?

I follow a structured approach: requirements gathering, planning, design mockups, development, testing, and deployment. I maintain regular communication throughout the project and provide updates on progress.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, I offer post-launch support and maintenance services. This includes bug fixes, minor updates, and technical support to ensure your website continues to perform optimally.

What technologies do you use?

I primarily work with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, SASS, and various modern web technologies. For backend development, I use PHP, MySQL, and WebSocket for real-time applications.
